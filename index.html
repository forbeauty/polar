Biography

Liming Zhang received her master's degree in the School of Software Engineering at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2023 with an academic scholarship. She interned at Intel Labs China from August to December 2021, where she was involved in the research of vision and language based dialog system. In 2022, she conducted her research under the supervision of Prof. Can Wang at the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), where she studied in the Intelligent Bionics Center. Her research interests focus on natural language understanding, 6DoF pose estimation for robotic arm grasping, and image processing.
